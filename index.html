  • A new paper is published from bioRxiv. (2020-08-24)
    This manuscript is now under review.

    Switching of OAS1 splicing isoforms mitigates SARS-CoV-2 infection
    IIDA K., Ajiro M., Muramoto Y., Takenaga T., Denawa M., Kurosawa R., Noda T., and Hagiwara M.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.1101/2021.08.23.457314
    This paper reports that manipulating RNA splicing of OAS1 with small molecule compounds enhances innate immunity in human cells, and provides the cells with resistance to SARS-CoV-2 virus infection.
